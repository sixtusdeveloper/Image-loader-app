Photo Loader App

The Photo Loader app enables users to upload or drag and drop photos, images, icons, and thumbnails of specific sizes. It resizes the images to fit the required dimensions and accepts files in the following extension formats: .jpeg, .png, .jpg. The app also creates a grid layout for display.
